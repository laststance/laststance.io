## Today's Highlight

**Security heads-up from [@phryneas](https://github.com/phryneas):** the bare `mui` and `redux-toolkit` npm namespaces are held by third parties — a baitsquatting risk for anyone fat-fingering an install. Always install scoped package names (`@mui/material`, `@reduxjs/toolkit`). See the [MUI issue](https://github.com/mui/material-ui/issues/48333) and the [Redux Toolkit issue](https://github.com/reduxjs/redux-toolkit/issues/5282).

**Runner-up:** [@kettanaito](https://github.com/kettanaito) tightening up MSW worker lifecycle — the ["still handling requests after stop"](https://github.com/mswjs/msw/pull/2724) fix should quietly resolve a bunch of flaky-test reports in the wild.
