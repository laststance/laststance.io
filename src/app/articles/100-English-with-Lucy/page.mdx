## 挨拶と会話の始め方

1. ****Hey, how are you?****
2. **How's everything going?**
3. **It's a pleasure to meet you.**
4. **Long time no see.**
5. **How have you been?**
6. **What are you up to these days?**

## 応答フレーズ

7. **I'm really good, thank you.**
8. **I'm doing well, thanks.**
9. **Not too bad.**
10. **Lovely to meet you too.**
11. **How about you?**
12. **Not much, the usual really.**

18. **It was so nice to meet you.**
